About Us

Motorbike Event Finder was created to allow bikers a place to find and advertise all events of the biking kind. Social media can be a great source of information and advertising but it can also be difficult to find the information as it is never in one place. Our aim is to simplify this and make finding up coming Rallies, Meet Ups, Charity Events or Club Hosted nights a quick and easy process.
